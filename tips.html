Travel Tips for Dhangadhi

Make your visit smooth, safe, and memorable

Tips for Travelers

  • Best time to visit: October to March for pleasant weather.
  • Local transport: Rickshaws, buses, and taxis are widely available.
  • Try local Tharu cuisine and street food for authentic flavors.
  • Respect local customs and cultural traditions.
  • Carry cash, as many small shops may not accept cards.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for exploring markets, parks, and riversides.
