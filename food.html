Taste Dhangadhi

Explore local Tharu cuisine (Dhikri), street food, and popular restaurants

Discover Food

Must-Try Dishes & Restaurants

Tharu Cuisine

Traditional dishes like Dhikri, Ghoghi, and local curry made by Tharu families.

Where: Tharu Cultural Village & local eateries

Street Food

Savor momos, chatpate, and sel roti at popular city markets.

Where: Local markets in Dhangadhi Bazaar

Local Restaurants

Family-run restaurants serving authentic Nepali and Tharu dishes.

Example: FFC (Fast Food Corner), Kafle Restaurant

Cafes and Lounge

Enjoy traditional Nepali tea, snacks, and relaxed ambiance at local cafes.

Where: City center and main streets like Galaxy Cafe, Gurung Sekuwa, and Chicken Station
