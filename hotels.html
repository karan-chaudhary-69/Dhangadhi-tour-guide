Recommended Hotels

Hotel Devotee Pvt. Ltd.

Located in the heart of Dhangadhi near the airport, Hotel Devotee is a premier, architecturally modern hotel featuring an elegant atrium design. It offers comfortable accommodations with essential amenities like a restaurant, bar, and 24/7 security, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers.

Hotel Pauwa Pvt. Ltd.

Situated in the heart of Dhangadhi, Hotel Pauwa is a hospitality-focused destination known for its blend of traditional Nepali service and modern comforts. The hotel offers air-conditioned rooms, free Wi-Fi, and top-tier amenities at affordable rates.

Hotel Jalsa Pvt. Ltd.

Located in the city center at Ratopool, Hotel Jalsa—whose name translates to a pavilion or stage for celebration—is a prominent 3-star property in far-western Nepal. It is set within a sprawling 35,473-square-foot estate characterized by its lush greenery and serene, natural atmosphere.

Hotel Rubus Pvt. Ltd.

Named after the Doteli word for "beautiful," Hotel Rubus is a premier hospitality destination located in Shivamarga, Baiyabehadi (Dhangadhi-2). It positions itself as the primary gateway for travelers exploring the riches of far-western Nepal, focusing on a philosophy of "serving people better."
