Top Attractions in Dhangadhi

Discover the beauty, culture, and hidden gems of the City of Love

Explore Now

Must-Visit Places

Shivapuri Dham

A revered spiritual site, Shivapuri Dham is surrounded by lush greenery and offers a tranquil retreat for visitors and devotees alike.

Dewhariya Botanical Garden

This peaceful garden boasts a variety of native flora, making it perfect for nature walks and picnics.

Jokher Taal

A scenic lake known for its serene waters and vibrant local birdlife, ideal for relaxation and photography.

Tharu Cultural Village

Experience the rich traditions of the Tharu people.

Dhangadhi Park

A perfect spot for families and nature lovers to relax in the heart of the city.

Mohona River

Relax by the serene river and enjoy scenic walks along its banks.

Local Market

Shop authentic Tharu handicrafts, local produce, and souvenirs.
