About Dhangadhi

The Heart of the Far-Western Terai – Rich in Culture and Love

Discover Dhangadhi

Dhangadhi is the largest city in the far western region of Nepal. Known as the "City of Love," it is home to the Tharu community, vibrant culture, and natural beauty. Visitors can explore rivers, parks, cultural villages, and local markets while enjoying the warm hospitality of the locals.

The city is also a hub for trade and education in the region, making it a unique mix of traditional and modern life.
